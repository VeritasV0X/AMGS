## 1. Overview

AMGS Final is a civic-design, policy-drafting, debate, and AI-assisted knowledge-building app. The app is designed to help users explore AMGS / Neolution ideas, save useful AI responses, and organize policy-related notes.

## 2. Information the app may process

Depending on the final build configuration, the app may process the following information:

- **Chat prompts:** Questions or text you type into the AI Chat feature.
- **AI responses:** Answers generated by the AI feature or local fallback system.
- **Saved chapters:** Titles, body text, section names, and notes you choose to save in the app.
- **App settings or local state:** Preferences or local data needed for the app to function.

The current intended design does not require account registration, advertising tracking, payment data, precise location, contacts, photos, health data, or access to other sensitive device data.

## 3. Local storage

Saved AMGS chapters and app content may be stored locally on your device using Apple's local storage technologies. Local saved content remains on your device unless you delete it, delete the app, enable a sync feature, or the final version is configured to transmit it elsewhere.

## 4. AI processing

If live AI is enabled, text you submit to the AI Chat feature may be sent to the configured AI provider to generate a response. This may include the prompt you typed and relevant app context needed to answer your request.

Do not enter sensitive personal information, secrets, passwords, financial account numbers, private health information, or other confidential data into the AI Chat.

If live AI is not enabled or fails, the app may use a built-in local fallback answer system.

## 5. iCloud or CloudKit

If the final version enables iCloud or CloudKit sync, saved app content may be synced through the user's Apple iCloud account according to Apple's iCloud terms and privacy practices. If iCloud sync is not used, remove this section and remove the iCloud capability/entitlements from the app before submission.

## 6. Analytics, ads, and tracking

The current intended version does not use third-party advertising, third-party ad tracking, or cross-app tracking. If analytics, crash reporting, ads, attribution SDKs, or tracking SDKs are added later, this policy and the App Store privacy responses must be updated before release.

## 7. Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only in these limited cases:

- With service providers needed to operate app features, such as an AI provider if live AI is enabled.
- If required to comply with law, legal process, or enforceable governmental request.
- To protect the rights, safety, or security of users, the developer, or others.

## 8. Data retention

Local saved content remains on your device until you delete it, delete the app, or reset the app data. Data processed by third-party service providers, such as an AI provider, is handled according to that provider's terms, data-processing settings, and retention policies.

## 9. User choices

You can avoid sending text to a live AI provider by not using AI Chat or by using a build where live AI is disabled. You can delete saved app content from within the app if a delete feature is available, or by deleting the app from your device.

## 10. Children

AMGS Final is not directed to children under 13. The app is intended for users interested in civic design, policy, debate, and governance concepts.

## 11. AI and accuracy notice

AI-generated content may be incomplete, inaccurate, outdated, or biased. The app should not be used as a substitute for professional legal, financial, medical, or governmental advice.
